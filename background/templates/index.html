Subtraction method
1.00
Isub = Isample − scale × Ibackground
Watches the Averaged folder(s) and subtracts each new sample automatically. Backgrounds are detected by name (buffer · blank · empty · solvent · water · bkg); every unsubtracted sample is paired with its nearest-index background, the scale is auto-set to zero the high-q residual (bounded near the high-q least-squares fit), and the result is written to Subtracted/. Files that still trip QC are saved as the best attempt and flagged in the manifest.
